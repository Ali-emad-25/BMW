About

It is a German multinational corporation that produces automobiles and motorcycles. The company was founded in 1916 as a manufacturer of aircraft engines, which it produced from 1917 to 1918 and again from 1933 to 1945. Cars are marketed under the BMW, Mini and Rolls-Royce brands, and motorcycles are marketed under the BMW Motorrad brand. It is one of the world's leading automobile companies and is the biggest competitor to other popular German automobile companies such as Mercedes, Audi, Volkswagen, Maybach and Smart. In 2015, BMW was the world's second-largest automobile manufacturer, with 2,279,503 vehicles produced.
